### MindPulse : A Mental Health Trend Analyzer for Early Detection and Intervention.

`Turning Emotional Data into Early Support`


1. What is the problem you are solving?

`I am solving the problem of hidden and poorly understood mental health struggles among teenagers and youth by using data to uncover trends, triggers and opportunuties for early intervention.`



2. What tools did you use?

` APIs` - (requests)Pull mental health data sets, connect to online surveys.


`TextBlob` - Analyze text responses, journal entries or social media post to detect positive and negative sentiments, anxiety indicators and emotional tones.


` Pandas` - Load surveys CSV files, organize questionanaire responses and structure mental health datasets.


` Geopandas` - Map mental health trends by region, school district, urban and rural areas.


`VS Code` - Full capstone build.


` Streamlit/ Tkinter` - Turn my analysis into an interactive web dashboard.




3. What insights did you or do you want to discover?/ What Solutions do you want to offer? Do People even need these solutions?

`INSIGHTS`
- `Major mental health triggers`: What factors are causing the most stress, anxiety or depression in teens and youths e.g academic pressure, cyberbullying, family instability etc.

- `High risk groups`: Which groups are most vulnerable?.

- `Emotional trends over time`: When do stress spikes happen? e.g exam season, post-graduation uncertainity, grief period, holiday loneliness etc.

- `Social media and emotional health correlation`: How does screen time affect mental wellness?.

- `Warning signs`: What behaviours may predict mental distress early? e.g isolation, negative language, insomnia/sleep loss, low motivation etc.


`SOLUTIONS`
- `Early warning dashboard`: My dashboard identifies high risk students, emotional stress spikes, trigger patterns etc.

- `Mental health resource recommendations`: Personalizes recommendations based on data ;counselling, stress management workshops, social media education, peer support groups etc.

- `Policy and school decision support`: Helps schools understand; when students need support most, which pressures are most harmful, where resources should go etc.

- `Awareness campaign strategy`: Use trend data to guide; NGO campaigns, parent education, youth wellness initiatives etc.


`IMPORTANCE`
- `Schools need it`: Teachers often see perfomance drops but not emotional causes.

- `Parents need it`: Many parents miss early warning signs.

- `Youth need it`: Many struggle silently due to stigma.

- `NGOs/Government need it`: Policy without data is often ineffective.

- `Real world needs it`: Mental health struggles are often invisible until crisis.
